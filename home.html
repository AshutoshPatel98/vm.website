About VM

That is part of the beauty of all literature. You discover that your longings are universal longings, that you're not lonely and isolated from anyone. You belong. Verba Maximus, BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus' annual national literary fest aims at bringing together all literature enthusiasts together in one place. With a series of exciting events and competitions, it is a platform for people to express their love for the language. Verba Maximus 2019 will be the ninth edition of the fest and promises 2 jam-packed days of thrilling events and entertainment. So mark your calendars, for the 19th and 20th of January and join us in celebrating the power of literature at BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus.
